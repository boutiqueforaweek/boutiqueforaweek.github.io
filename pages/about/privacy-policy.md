**Effective Date: March 20, 2026**

We are committed to protecting your privacy at Boutique For A Week. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it. The information you may provide includes:

* **Newsletter subscription**: When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect your email address. Our newsletter is managed by [Brevo](https://www.brevo.com/) (formerly Sendinblue). By subscribing, your email is processed in accordance with [Brevo's Privacy Policy](https://www.brevo.com/en/legal/privacypolicy/).
* **Consignor and volunteer registration**: When you register as a consignor or volunteer, information is collected through our registration partner [MySaleManager.net](https://www.mysalemanager.net/), which is governed by their own privacy policy.

## Cookies and Analytics

* **Google Analytics (GA4)**: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This service collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location. Google Analytics may set cookies on your browser. You can opt out by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
* **Cloudflare Turnstile**: Our newsletter subscription form uses Cloudflare Turnstile for spam protection. This service may set cookies and process data in accordance with [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
* **Newsletter cookie**: We set a cookie named "newsletter" to remember whether you have seen the newsletter signup popup, so we do not show it repeatedly.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

* Send you updates about upcoming sales and events
* Process your consignor or volunteer registration
* Improve our website and user experience

## Data Sharing

We are the sole owner of all information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties, except as described above with our service providers (Brevo, MySaleManager.net, Google Analytics, Cloudflare) who process data on our behalf.

## Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email. To request deletion of your data, please [contact us](mailto:{{ site.email }}).

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }}).
