Have you heard? Consignor registration is open for our Spring 2016 Sale. [Register](src/pages/register.md) soon before all our slots fill up!

You can also [register](https://www.mysalemanager.net/wrk_start.aspx?partnercode=BFAW) as a [Volunteer](src/pages/volunteers/index.md), schedule your [Volunteer shift](https://www.mysalemanager.net/wrk_readonlyworkshifts.aspx?partnercode=BFAW) or [Drop Off appointment](https://www.mysalemanager.net/drp_start.aspx?partnercode=BFAW), and (if applicable) [sign up](https://www.mysalemanager.net/mom_start.aspx?partnercode=BFAW) as an [Expecting/New/Adopting/Foster Mom](src/pages/shoppers/first-time-moms.md) to shop early!

We look forward to seeing our returning Consignors and welcoming first time Consignors to the sale.
